QUESTION MARK?

My mental state is confused, looking for answers my
mind has questioned;
If there is a superior entity,is it the one the holy
book has mentioned?

My parents take me to temples, tell me to join hands,
bow and pray;
I wonder if the statue that stands,holds the entity with
a positive ray?

Every religion has different god, every caste does too;
Sometimes i wonder, wasn't this convenient for book creators to do?

The gods in the book belong to different caste and
also different religion;
I wonder, what's the difference then, if humans do
the same stupid division?

There is always a big question mark,when i close
my eyes and bow;
I would like to connect to the superior entity, but the question is how?
