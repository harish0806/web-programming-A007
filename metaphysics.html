What is Metaphysics?

Metaphysics is a branch of philosophy investigating the fundamental
nature of being and the world that encompasses it.

The question that arises in an individuals mind are:

  • What is existance?
  • Is god real?
  • What is the meaning to life?
  • What is our significance?
