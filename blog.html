ISLAND

The sand, the forest and the ocean around, oh my it seems
so beautiful;
Nature's beauty offers you comfort, but soon it turns
so awful.

The forest is so beautiful, seems like nature is at
its best;
Well it's too violent in reality,heart beats racing
inside your chest.

The ocean seems so blue as a pearl, the waves running
to the shore;
When thirst leaves you all high and dry, the ocean gives
no escape door.

The island looks so fantastic and great, a perfect
looking place;
However you don't realize the pain and violence, the island
withholds inside the closed case.
